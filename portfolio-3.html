The Lorax Initiative

Design Proposal

Description

  • Shruti Goyal

  • Final brochure

  • January 2021 - June 2021

The Lorax Initiative proposed a series of interactive art exhibits on campus focused on visualizing and sonifying plant data to promote plant health. We utilized JXCT Soil NPK Sensors to measure nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels in the soil, along with KeeYees Soil Moisture Sensor Modules to collect data on watering schedules. We created a proposal and prototype, which we presented to the team at the Het Nieuwe Instituut.
